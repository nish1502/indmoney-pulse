### Action Ideas
1. [HIGH] **Restore EPF/NPS Manual Tracking & Clarify MF NAV Updates:**
   Address user frustration and potential financial tracking errors by reintroducing manual tracking for EPF/NPS and providing clear daily MF NAV update timings.

2. [MEDIUM] **Investigate and Resolve UPI Payment Failures:**
   Prioritize fixing the critical issue where UPI payments are failing, leading to debits without credits, to prevent user financial loss and restore trust.

3. [MEDIUM] **Revert External Portfolio Refresh to Previous OTP Method:**
   Improve user experience and reduce friction by rolling back the complex QR code upload for external portfolio refresh to the simpler, previously functional OTP method.

4. [LOW] **Review and Clarify US Stock Account Creation for NRIs:**
   Address misleading information by clearly outlining the process or limitations for NRI US account creation within the app.
